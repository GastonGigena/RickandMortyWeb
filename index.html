Sinopsis

Rick and Morty (in English: Rick and Morty) is an American animated television series for adults created by Justin Roiland and Dan Harmon in 2013 for Adult Swim, also aired on Cartoon Network. The series follows the misadventures of a scientist, Rick Sanchez, and his easily influenced grandson, Morty, who balance their time between domestic life and space, time and intergalactic travel. Dan Harmon, the co-creator of the series and Justin Roiland are responsible for the main voices of Morty and Rick, the series also includes the voices of Chris Parnell, Spencer Grammer and Sarah Chalke. The series originated from a scrappy short animated by Roiland for the Channel 101 film festival called Doc and Mharti, a parody of the main leads from Back to the Future. When Harmon was fired by NBC in 2013 over arguments between him and the executives of the series he had created, Community, he and Roiland developed a series based on the characters already created by Roiland.

About me

My name is Fernando Gastón Gigena Parola, I am an independent graphic and advertising designer dedicated to the visual communication in different formats and supports. I have fifteen years of experience graphically solving the communication needs of people, who trust and decide to delegate to my profession the image of their company, product or service.

At this moment I am carrying out this project for Argentina Programa 4.0, a national and federal program that aims to train people in languages and knowledge about programming, testing and digital skills to enhance employability in the software and technology sector. The program is promoted by the Ministry of Economy of the Nation with the support of a specialized advisory council and its main objectives are:

  • Train more than 70,000 people over the course of a year so that they can acquire knowledge and tools related to the software industry and promote their job placement in the sector.

  • Articulate the needs of the market with the training of more people in technology.

  • Generate added value to traditional and non-traditional industrial sectors.
